Manager capabilities

  • GPS navigation

    You will be aware of all the movements of the sales team. You can also keep track of which points have or have not been visited.

  • Sales planning and KPI

    The system recommends a sales plan and gives a forecast for each day so that you can meet your plans. You will also be able to set up your KPIs and calculate sales reps earnings.

  • One-stop window for all affiliates

    You can create an unlimited number of affiliates and manage them from single place. Get consolidated reports for all affiliates in a one-stop window.

  • Warehouse management

    Browse recommended stock based on sales history. You can also find out the balances for a certain date and get a material report.

Sales representative capabilities

  • Debts

    The sales rep has the ability to browse the debt of each client or the general list of debtors.

  • Reports and KPIs

    Browse daily and monthly reports, KPI progress, as well as the amount of earnings.

  • Client history

    The application creates individual profiles for each of your clients and provides you access to information about what products your client purchased, since when your client has been in debt and how much they owe.

  • Warehouse

    The app helps you to browse the availability of goods in stock online.

What about forwarder?

  • GPS route

    The application allows you to arrange a route at the beginning of the day and check for the entire list of outlets scheduled for the day.

  • Acceptance of payment

    After receiving the payment, the employee enters the data into the application, and at the end of the business day, the software provides a report on the funds received.

  • Order status

    The forwarder has the opportunity to directly change the order if the client decides to purchase a certain product or for some reason the product arrived incomplete.

  • List of debtors

    The freight forwarder has the ability to browse the debt of each client or the general list of debtors, as well as upload a reconciliation report.
